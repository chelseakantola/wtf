`1` Toggle Drawer

`2` Focus Omnibar 

`F` Navigate to Friends stream

`N` Navigate to Noise stream

`R` Open notifications

`=` Toggle grid mode for main content

`+` Toggle grid mode for drawer content

`?` Show help modal

`CMD/CTRL + Enter` Submit a post or comment.
